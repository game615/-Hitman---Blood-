## 📖 About Hitman: Blood Money

**Hitman: Blood Money** is a stealth-action video game developed by IO Interactive and published by Eidos Interactive. As the fourth main installment in the iconic Hitman series, the game follows **Agent 47**, a genetically engineered assassin working for the International Contract Agency (ICA).

Players travel across various international locations, eliminating targets through carefully planned operations. The game emphasizes player choice, allowing missions to be completed in multiple ways, from silent assassinations to direct confrontations.

Renowned for its sandbox-style mission design, Hitman: Blood Money remains a fan-favorite entry in the franchise.

## 🕹️ Gameplay Overview

In **Hitman: Blood Money**, each mission is designed as an open sandbox, encouraging players to study targets, observe routines, and determine the most effective assassination methods.

Players can:

* Use disguises to blend into environments.
* Create accidents to eliminate targets discreetly.
* Employ stealth tactics to avoid detection.
* Utilize environmental objects and distractions.
* Upgrade weapons and customize equipment.
* Replay missions to discover alternative strategies.

Every decision impacts mission outcomes and overall notoriety.
